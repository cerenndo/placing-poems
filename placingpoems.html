Placing Poems.

HOME INSTRUCTIONS THE MAP CONTACT

In April 2021, Placing Poems offered the chance to write about new and imagined journeys away from the stifling reality of the pandemic. We combined all the contributions into one map, representing many poetic journeys, may they be grand or small, real or imagined, taken or anticipated. Together with our contributors, we created a poetic cartography of anticipated adventures for everyone to enjoy for Poetry Day Ireland and beyond. Click on a marker to read a poem! Zoom out to explore more journeys.
